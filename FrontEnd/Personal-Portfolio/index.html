About Me


Hi, I’m Pawan Ingle, an aspiring Full-Stack Web Developer with a strong foundation in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js) and a growing command over SQL and NoSQL databases like PostgreSQL and MongoDB.


I’m currently pursuing my Master of Computer Applications (MCA) at Chandigarh University, driven by a deep interest in building scalable, efficient, and user-centric web applications.


Before moving into software development, I worked in operations and tech-support-oriented roles at companies like ETS.


These experiences taught me how to work with precision, think critically, and communicate clearly—skills I now apply directly to my development projects.


I’m looking for collaborative opportunities to contribute to meaningful software products, learn from experienced teams, and grow as a full-stack engineer. If you're working on something exciting or looking for a developer who’s eager to grow, let’s connect!
